Display your activity on [GiantBomb](http://giantbomb.com) or your GamerGrade card in a customizable widget.

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin archive and expand it.
1. Put the ‘giantbomb-widget’ folder into your wp-content/plugins/ directory.
1. Go to the Plugins page in your WordPress Administration area and click ‘Activate’ for GiantBomb Widget.
1. Go to the Widgets page for your theme and add the widget to a sidebar.
1. Fill in your GiantBomb.com username and pick what you want to display in the widget.
